Election Phases

1

Voter Registration

Ensure you are eligible and on the Electoral Roll updated by the ECI.

2

Nomination & MCC

ECI announces dates (MCC begins). Candidates file nomination papers.

3

Campaigning

Rallies and manifestos. Campaigning ends 48 hours before polling.

4

Polling Day

Voters cast their ballots securely using Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs).

5

Counting & Results

EVMs are opened, votes are counted, and ECI declares the official results.
